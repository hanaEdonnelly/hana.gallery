Advising for Fall 2022

Advising for Fall 2022 Honors courses opens March 1. Registration links are sent to your SC email. Emails are never sent from GMail or other unofficial addresses.
We look forward to meeting with each student every semester to ensure your success. Through reflective conversations, an Honors advisor will encourage exploration of your academic interests and identify opportunities that help fulfill degree and honors requirements. Honors advisors strive to incorporate the principles of Appreciative Advising in appointments with you. This means they will ask positive, open-ended questions that help you optimize your educational experience and achieve your dreams, goals and potentials.
