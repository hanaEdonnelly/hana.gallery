Applications due for Washington semester program

Time is running out to apply to the SCHC Washington semester Program!
While in this program, students will live, work, and study in Washington, D.C. to prepare for careers in the political sphere. Students will work in a variety of roles with the South Carolina Congressional Delegation.
This program is not just limited to those majoring and minoring, and we invite all those who are interested to apply now!
