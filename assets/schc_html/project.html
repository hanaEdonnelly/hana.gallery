This website was created to fulfull requirements outlined by Prof. Travis Dalton at the University of South Carolina for ITEC362: Intro to Web Systems. The subject matter of this website is solely used for this classwork example and does not represent the University of South Carolina, its colleges, or interests.

The goal with this website was to envision a redesign of the South Carolina Honors College website to be more user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing.
